ABOUT US

PromptEQUATION was born from the desire to build innovative and useful solutions for business, boosted by the open source community. Founded in 2015 as a result of 18 years of experience in developing software and technologies, our client will always be able to access the newest technological tools.

In this changeable world of the digital where the speed is paramount, creativity and innovation should be valued and encouraged. Therefore, we only recruit the best professionals, promote their creativity and offer guidance in order to guarantee excellency in our services. Our team works both locally and remotely and we manage it by providing the tools, methods, knowledge and the training to push them towards success.

At PromptEQUATION, we develop and manage tools with our business in mind. Different companies have different needs and we offer tailored solutions to fit your business now and in the future. We have a strong foundation and structure of practices and tools which enables us to work on complex projects everyday. Our goal is to provide the best solutions and accomodate them to the new industry's demands in a forever mutating environment.

Vision

PromptEquation was created from the Open Source movement, therefore we believe the future of technology is dependent on collaboration. We learn together and discuss the best methods and practices in order to be better developers.

For our customers that means estabilishing trustworthy relationships by continuously improving ourselves, our methods and processes.

For us it means to be considered by our clients and employees as a reliable and respected company in the industry for the quality and the innovation of our products.

Values

We adapt to our customers needs, developing tools that will help them - not tools that can found anywhere else on the market.

To us, transparency goes beyond coding. We take that mindset and apply it to other aspects in the company.

As a team, we value each and everyone's contribution and make sure our employess can achieve their full potential through knowledge sharing.

We fully support the Open Source movement by actively participating and contribute to the community.

"Managing my project was hell 'till I found you guys"

That quote is our motto and always music to our ears!

Mission

At PromptEQUATION our goal is to keep our clients satisfied and exceed their expectations by:

  1. Delivering high quality products;
  2. Construct technological solutions with organizational development in mind;
  3. Continue innovating and being in the forefront of technology.

And offer our employees:

  1. The possibility to adjust work to their personal needs. We care about our employees' well-being and working remotely or locally is each individual's choice, but responsibility is a must;
  2. Training and provide tools to attain success;
  3. Recognition of each individual's contribution and experience.
