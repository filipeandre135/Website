ABOUT US

PromptEQUATION was born from the desire to build innovative and useful solutions for business, boosted by the open source community. Founded in 2015 as a result of 18 years of experience in developing software and technologies, our client will always be able to access the newest technological tools.

In this changeable world of the digital where the speed is paramount, creativity and innovation should be valued and encouraged. Therefore, we only recruit the best professionals, promote their creativity and offer guidance in order to guarantee excellency in our services. Our team works both locally and remotely and we manage it by providing the tools, methods, knowledge and the training to push them towards success.

At PromptEQUATION, we develop and manage tools with our business in mind. Different companies have different needs and we offer tailored solutions to fit your business now and in the future. We have a strong foundation and structure of practices and tools which enables us to work on complex projects everyday. Our goal is to provide the best solutions and accomodate them to the new industry's demands in a forever mutating environment.
